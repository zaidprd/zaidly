### Burr Material: Steel vs. Ceramic

Never buy a ceramic burr grinder if you can afford steel. Stainless steel burrs are sharper, faster, and produce the clarity required for pour-over. Ceramic burrs "crush" the beans, while steel "cuts" them.

### Adjustment Mechanism

Look for grinders with clear "click" settings or external adjustment rings. For pour-over, you want a grinder that allows you to repeat settings easily so you can swap between a V60 and a larger Chemex brew without guesswork.
